About

I've had a passion for computers since the late 2000's, spending almost all my time figuring out the many things I could do with one. Although the start of my programming passion began at age 6 where I first learnt the language Lua on the now popular gaming platform, ROBLOX, in 2011.


Seeing how others made captivating games inspired me to give a shot at it, leading me into various aspects of game design, including 3D Modelling, Graphics, Motion Design and more. At an early age, I was collaborating with other likeminded young people in order to make experiences others can enjoy.


This early passion has led me nowadays to pursue Computer Science at GCSE, A-Level and Degree level, as the subject continues to intrigue me at every step of the way. I aspire to put my theoretical and practical knowledge altogether to good use in the technology industry.
